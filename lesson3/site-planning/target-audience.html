Target Audience Description

My target auidence is youth from the age of 12-18. My target auidence is young students who have a desire to learn more about science, specifically about weather and how it works.

Personas

- David Jones is a 9 year old who lives in Nampa Idaho. He is facinated by baremetric changes and the sudden weather changes. He loves science and graphs. It often switches from windy to warm and dry where he lives.

- Lisa Madsen is a 16-year-old living in Panhandle Florida. She has experienced two hurricans in her life and his fasicinated with them. She lives in a rural area and doesn't get weather radio channels. She wants to know when a hurrican is coming. She is also interested in learning how huurricans are formed and why they accure.
