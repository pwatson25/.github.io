Annual Winter Fest

Soda Springs has another eventful time at the annual end of Winter Fest! With residents from all around Idaho and even Washighton, Colorado, and Utah, this event is surely spectacular. Nothing beats tubbing down this hot spring in the cold of winter. It's nice and cozy! That is untill you have to get out. At the end there is hot chocolate, warm fires, and some winter games! This is truly an amazing once a year event. But you can come back and enjoy the hot springs anytime of the year!
